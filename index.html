FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

Who do you work with — individuals, businesses, or both?
Both. We prepare returns for individuals, families, retirees, contractors, self-employed filers, and businesses of all sizes — including LLCs, S-corps, and partnerships.
Do you handle Kentucky local occupational taxes?
Yes. We prepare Fayette County and other Kentucky local occupational tax returns, which are often overlooked — and sometimes the source of unexpected penalties when missed.
What if I'm behind on filing or owe back taxes?
We can help. We prepare prior-year returns, help you understand your options with the IRS and KDOR, and can assist with payment plans. There's no judgment — just a plan to get you square.
How do consultations work?
Your first conversation is always free. We offer in-person meetings at our Lexington office, phone calls, and video consultations — whatever works best for your schedule.
Can you help me set up a new business?
Absolutely. We help new business owners choose the right entity (LLC, S-corp, sole proprietor), register with the state, set up payroll, and build good bookkeeping habits from day one.
What are your fees?
Fees vary by the complexity of your return and the services you need. We provide clear, upfront quotes — no hourly surprises or hidden charges. Contact us for a free estimate.
