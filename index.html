About Town Branch Tax

A more Kentucky take on the modern tax office.

Instead of looking generic or overly corporate, this concept leans into Bluegrass identity: warm cream tones, navy and bourbon-gold accents, a subtle flowing creek motif, and language that sounds grounded and local.

  • Lexington feel: heritage-inspired typography and a calm, established visual tone.
  • Simple stack: single HTML file, embedded CSS, no framework required.
  • Easy to maintain: ideal for GitHub Pages and easy future edits.
Why this direction works

Closer to Lexington than a generic template.

More local character

Use references to the Bluegrass, horse country, limestone, town branches, and family-run Kentucky businesses.

More warmth

Swap cold corporate stock styling for cream backgrounds, serif headlines, and a more personal voice.

More trust

Keep the page structure straightforward: hero, services, about, FAQ, and contact—nothing flashy or hard to maintain.

FAQ

Do you work with individuals or only businesses?

Both. This layout supports individual returns, family tax prep, and business services without needing separate pages at launch.

Can this stay simple on GitHub Pages?

Yes. This concept is intentionally one static page with no build process, database, or JavaScript dependency.

Could this expand later?

Absolutely. You can later split Services, About, and Contact into separate pages while keeping the same visual identity.
